About Me

Hi, I'm Wycliffe Mwanganda

I'm an innovator, builder, and lifelong learner. I love creating useful tools, gadgets, and applications — sometimes things people didn't even realize they needed! I've already built projects like a student report generator tool in Excel, and even crafted simple engineering projects like gloves with built-in flashlights.

This website is my space to document what I've made, what I'm learning, and what I'm dreaming up next. I plan to explore everything from cybersecurity, programming, video editting, animation, electronics, — to building real-world devices based on my ideas.

I'll be sharing tutorials, lessons learned, and behind-the-scenes looks at how I tackle each project. It's all part of my journey to grow my skills — and hopefully inspire others to start their own.

When I'm not building, learning or experimenting, I spend time with my family, but while doing all that it's all spent with God.
